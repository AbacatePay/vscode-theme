Green-first theme for IntelliJ IDEs, based on the AbacatePay visual identity.

## Installation
</div>

1. Open your IntelliJ IDE.
2. Go to **Settings / Preferences** ‚Üí **Editor** ‚Üí **Color Scheme**.
3. Click the **‚öôÔ∏è Gear icon** next to the scheme selector.
4. Choose **Import Scheme** ‚Üí **IntelliJ color scheme (.icls) or JSON (.json)**.
5. From this repository, select one of the following files:
   - `editors/intellij/abacatepay-theme-dark.json`
   - `editors/intellij/abacatepay-theme-light.json`
   - `editors/intellij/abacatepay-theme-soft.json`

6. Click **Apply**, then **OK**.
